# Dad's Birthday Sites

An annual tradition: every year my dad gets a birthday website. Three of them now — dad-birthday, dad-turns-51, dad-turns-52 — each a self-contained page you open on his birthday and hand him the phone.

## How it works

Each one is a single `index.html`: a full-screen canvas starfield behind a giant gradient age number ("52") that scales and rotates in, an italic "Dad," and a tappable cake. Six photos live in a `photos/` folder per site. Same warm gold-on-black template each year, restyled and re-shot — the earliest one has a few scaffold variants (`index.basic.html`, `index.standalone.html`) from figuring out the format. No framework, no build; it's meant to load instantly on a phone.

## Stack

Single-file HTML, Canvas, CSS animation

## Status

Three shipped, one per birthday. The streak continues.
