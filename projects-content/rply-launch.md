# rply-launch

The RPLY launch package: Product Hunt listing, Show HN post, and organic X posts, written and ready — plus the session RESUME doc that captures the whole howdynox campaign state (DNS records, Vercel projects, redirect chain) so any future session can pick it up cold.

## How it works
Two markdown files. `LAUNCH.md` has the full PH kit (tagline, description, first maker comment) aimed at Mac developers, zero spam, everything gated on founder sign-off — it's her product. `RESUME.md` is the operational snapshot: what's live, what's blocked, and the exact `claude --resume` ID to reopen the working session.
