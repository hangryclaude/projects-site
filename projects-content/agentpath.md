# Agentpath

A lead-capture site for selling custom AI agents to businesses. One page, one job: convince a visitor an agent could do their busywork, then get their email.

## How it works

Next.js App Router, one long page built from sections — hero with a gradient shader, an agent-spec configurator, a live-feeling agent demo, worked examples, and a contact form that posts to an API route. Magnetic buttons and scroll reveals do the polish. No CMS, no dashboard; the whole thing is the pitch.

## Stack

TypeScript, Next.js, React, Tailwind

## Status

Built, not wired to a real backend beyond the contact route. Prototype.
