# Agent·SB

Landing page for a hyper-local business idea: I come to your house in Santa Barbara, install a custom AI agent on your computer, and teach you how to use it. You fill out a form saying what you'd use it for; I call you back.

## How it works

One static HTML file. The intake form posts to Formspree (falls back to a pre-filled mailto if no form ID is set, so no lead is ever lost). Pricing deliberately not shown — that conversation happens on the phone.

## Stack

HTML, CSS, vanilla JS, Formspree

## Status

Built, not launched. The service behind it never got its first customer.
