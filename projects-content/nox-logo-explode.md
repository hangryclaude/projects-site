# nox-logo-explode

Two more ways to destroy the NOX logo: "detonate" (`index.html`) and "glass shatter" (`shatter.html`). Brand-asset play for the NOX socials — the logo blows apart or shatters like a pane on click.

## How it works
Two standalone HTML files plus the logo PNG. Canvas-rendered particle and shard effects, no dependencies, open directly in a browser.

## Stack
HTML, vanilla JS, Canvas

## Status
Working one-shot toy; sibling of nox-explode.
