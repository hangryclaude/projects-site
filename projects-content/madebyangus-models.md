# madebyangus Models

The 3D model library behind the madebyangus portfolio site — laptops, computers, and monitors sourced from Kenney and Creative Trio's low-poly packs.

## How it works

A curated asset repo, not code. `RECOMMENDATIONS.md` and `SUMMARY.txt` document which model is used where on the site, so swapping one out doesn't mean re-auditing everything.

## Stack

Blender, glTF assets (Kenney, Creative Trio)

## Status

Asset repo, in use by madebyangus.
