# Larp Shopify

"Maple & Stone Co." — a complete Shopify-looking storefront for a store that does not exist. A LARP store: product grid, branding, the works, all mock.

## How it works

Single self-contained `index.html`, no backend, no build. Open it in a browser and you're shopping at a home-goods brand that has never shipped a single item.

## Stack

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, one file.

## Status

Live at larp-shopify.vercel.app. Still not a real store.
