NOX Bullet

NOX & RPLYJavaScript2026
GitHub ↗ Live ↗

An interactive hero for the NOX brand: a bullet fires into the logo. Click or hit space to fire again. That's the whole product, and that's why it works — one violent, satisfying interaction instead of a paragraph of copy.

How it works

Zero-dependency canvas. No three.js, no libraries — the bullet, impact, and logo are drawn and animated straight on a 2D canvas, so the entire thing loads instantly and runs anywhere. Built with the crazy-web-effects skill family, which is where the motion craft comes from.

Stack

JavaScript, HTML canvas — no dependencies, no build step

Status

Live at hangryclaude.github.io/nox-bullet.
