Shopify Sim

Commerce & hustlesHTML2026
GitHub ↗

A fake Shopify sales dashboard that looks real and feels real: revenue ticking up, orders rolling in from Tokyo and Brooklyn, and — the good part — actual push notifications landing on your phone.

How it works

One dark glassmorphic index.html simulates live sales for the Underground805 catalog at four speeds (trickle → insane). pinger.js runs standalone and fires real notifications through ntfy.sh, so your phone buzzes "cha-ching" like the store is popping off. Built as motivation fuel, or a prank, depending on who's holding the phone.

Stack

Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, Node, ntfy.sh push.

Status

Working. It is not, to be clear, real money.
