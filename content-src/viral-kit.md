# viral-kit 🚀

Launch assets for taking the **github.com/hangryclaude** ("HANGRY") repos public —
ethically (no bought followers, no fake stars) while protecting anonymity.

## Contents

- **`LAUNCH.md`** — the master launch playbook (the core thesis + step-by-step plan).
- **`TWEETS.md`** — ready-to-post tweet drafts.
- **`SCRUB.md`** — the pre-public scrub checklist (strip PII / private business / secrets).
- **`README-*.md`** — polished per-project launch READMEs (face-fx, habit-warden, term-grid).

Start with `SCRUB.md` before anything goes public, then work `LAUNCH.md`.
